Experimental Procedure


Step

Description

Image

1

Prepared 30 LB agar plates, three light boxes, and six liquid cultures. Each experimental group had two liquid cultures.

2

1 mL of LB broth added to each tube and placed in a water bath at 37°C overnight.

3

Cultures exposed to one of the three light conditions under a light box depending on their experimental group.

4

Each culture used to inoculate a plate with three ampicillin disks.

5

Plates incubated upside down overnight at 37°C.

6

The diameters of the zones of inhibition measured with a ruler and recorded. Steps 2-6 were repeated for 5 rounds with the same cultures, compounding the light exposure.

7

Data organized, analyzed, and graphed in Google Sheets.
